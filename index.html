Why Your Small Business Needs a
Website

By Fiverr Team | April 13, 2023

No matter how big or small your business is or what industry your company falls under, a website is necessary to be seen and heard in today's competitive world. Digital marketing is the go-to way to advertise and market to new and existing customers.

A strong and established online presence will give you an edge over the competition, find new business, better manage existing customers, and ultimately make you more money. Plus, it's easier than ever to create a website. There are many tools to help you, including a pool of talented freelancers at Fiverr that you can hire to do all the work for you.

Reasons to Have a Website for Your Company

If you're still wondering, does my business need a website? The answer is Y-E-S! Here are the most important reasons why your company needs a website and how you can make it happen.

1. Increase Visibility

Most people looking for a product or service will search online before looking elsewhere. Yet, only 71% of businesses have a website. So, how will anyone know where to find you if you're not in the rankings? In today's digital age, an online presence is essential for any business, large or small. An up-to-date website allows you to establish your company digitally, making it easier for customers to find you online and connect with you more effectively.

2. Reach a Wider Audience

Reach a wider audience than ever before with traditional marketing channels. A quality-built website with solid SEO keyword utilization links to social media, and other important information about your business will help you reach a global audience, allowing you to grow outside your local community. What does that mean? More customers and more sales .

3. Showcase Products and Services

Having a website for your small business allows you to create and manage avirtual store where you can showcase and sell your products and services. From t-shirts to yoga classes, you can offer just about anything, from physical products to a wide range of services. Plus, a website is always accessible, unlike a physical store or office with limited hours. This allows potential customers to learn more about your products and services anytime.


What's Involved in Creating a Small Business Website

Sure. Having a website for your brand sounds like a great idea, but what does it entail, and how complicated (and time-consuming) is it? Here's what you should do when
developing a small business website:

1. Choose and acquire a domain name that is relevant and easy to remember.

2. Gather digital assets, including product photos, videos, written copy, and other content.

3. Find the best website host with dependable support, such as WordPress or GoDaddy.

4. Research e-commerce the best one for your business, such as Shopify or Wix.

5. Create an easy-to-navigate user visitors to browse your business site seamlessly.

6. Optimize your entire website for search engines, including product and page descriptions.

7. Continually manage and update to all social media and other online platforms.
